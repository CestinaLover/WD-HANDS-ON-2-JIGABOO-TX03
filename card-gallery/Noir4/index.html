MASTER CHIEF

About Master Chief

Master Chief, or John-117, is a supersoldier and the central hero of the Halo series. Trained from childhood and enhanced with advanced technology, he is humanity's ultimate weapon against the Covenant and other intergalactic threats. Equipped with the resilient Mjolnir armor and aided by the AI Cortana, Master Chief's leadership and combat skills are unparalleled, making him the last line of defense for humanity.
