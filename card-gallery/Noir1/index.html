HEATBLAST

About Heatblast

Heatblast, hailing from the planet Pyros, is a fiery alien capable of manipulating intense heat and flames. As one of the primary transformations of Ben Tennyson's Omnitrix, Heatblast uses his pyrokinetic abilities to fight against evil forces, protecting the universe with his blazing attacks and fiery determination.
