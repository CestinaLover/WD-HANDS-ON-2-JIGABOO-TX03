KRATOS

About Kratos

Kratos, known as the Ghost of Sparta, is a demigod warrior of immense strength and ferocity. Formerly bound to the service of the Olympian gods, Kratos is driven by a burning desire for vengeance. Armed with the Blades of Chaos and fueled by his Spartan Rage, he relentlessly battles gods, titans, and monsters alike in his quest for redemption and revenge.
